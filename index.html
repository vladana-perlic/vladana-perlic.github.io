Vladana Perlić

Based in Grenoble, France.

Industrial PhD researcher in Generative AI and NLP. Poet, writer, and literary translator. This site gathers my research papers, books, awarded poems, translations, interviews, and a new popular-science manuscript about AI misconceptions.

About

I study AI through language, evidence, and literature.

I am an Industrial PhD researcher at STMicroelectronics and Télécom Paris, working on LLMs, retrieval-augmented generation, agentic workflows, and multimodal technical documentation. I also write poetry, prose, and literary translations, mostly in Serbian and across translation.

That double life is the method, not the detour: literature trained me to notice ambiguity, tone, silence, and what a text refuses to say; AI research keeps me asking what systems retrieve, what they invent, and how people decide whether an answer deserves trust.

Location
