Vladana Perlić

Based in Grenoble, France.

Industrial PhD researcher in Generative AI and NLP. Poet, writer, and literary translator. I research, write, translate, publish, collect prizes, and make jokes precisely when adults would advise against it. This site gathers the receipts: AI papers, books, awarded poems, translations, interviews, and a new popular-science manuscript on AI misconceptions.

About

I write poems and study machines that need to lay off the shrooms

I am an Industrial PhD researcher at STMicroelectronics and Télécom Paris, working on LLMs, retrieval-augmented generation, agentic workflows, and multimodal technical documentation. In less CV-shaped terms: I study how AI systems find information, assemble answers, improvise when they should not, and sound frighteningly calm while doing it.

I also write poetry, prose, essays, and popular science articles. The overlap is simple: I spend a lot of time asking what a sentence is doing, what it is leaving out, and whether there is evidence behind the confidence. The awards are here, politely pretending I do not enjoy them, if anyone needs proof that the jokes come with follow-through.
